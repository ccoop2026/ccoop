Product Requirements Document (PRD): ccoop.in MVP

1. Project Overview

ccoop.in is a multi-campus, mobile-first e-commerce platform designed for college merchandise. The platform utilizes a unique "Soft Launch" pre-order model to validate demand and minimize inventory risk through Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) targets.

2. Core Architecture

  • Multi-Tenancy: Single platform hosting multiple independent campus storefronts.
  • Contextual Onboarding: Users must select a campus to enter a specific store context (persistent via cookies/profiles).
  • User Roles: Student/Alumni (End-user), Drop-Host (On-campus logistics), Campus Curator (Store management), and Super Admin (Global ops).

3. The Pre-Order Campaign Lifecycle (The "Drop")

The platform's primary innovation is a 5-stage campaign flow:

Phase 1: The Pledge (Soft Launch)

Phase 2: The Green Light (Conversion)

Triggered when the campaign reaches its target. Users receive a notification with a payment link valid for a limited window (e.g., 24-48h). Dev Requirement: Automated SMS/Email/WhatsApp triggers upon reaching MOQ.

Phase 3: Token Payment (Commitment)

Users pay a 50% "Token Advance" to secure their order. Dev Requirement: Robust payment gateway integration with support for partial payments and "Balance Due" tracking in order history.

Phase 4: Production & Tallying

Admin manages factory vendors and stock arrival. Dev Requirement: Warehouse/Drop-Host dashboard for scanning barcodes to tally incoming factory stock against expected orders.

Phase 5: Final Settlement & Pickup

Users are notified when items arrive at the campus "Drop Host." Final 50% balance must be paid digitally to unlock the "Pickup QR Code." Dev Requirement: QR scanning logic that validates payment status before marking an item as "Dispatched."

4. Key Business Logic & Edge Cases

  • Variant-Level MOQ: Campaigns can succeed at the product level but fail at the color level.
  • Failure Handling: Logic for issuing 110% store credit or UPI refunds if MOQ is not met.
  • Admin Overrides: Ability for Super Admins to "Force Production" even if targets are slightly missed.


5. Project Screens

  • Campus Selection: Landing page for selecting a specific campus store.
  • Product Gallery: Grid view of active 'Drops' and product categories.
  • Product Detail Page: Detailed view with 'Pledge' button and real-time progress.
  • User Dashboard: Tracking pledges, payment history, and pickup QR codes.
  • Admin Panel: Management interface for MOQ tracking and logistics.
