Product Requirements Document: MVP Launch

Building a community-driven commerce platform focused on collective purchasing power and optimized retail logistics.

Project Overview

The ccoop.in MVP aims to validate the core loop of community commerce: pooling demand to unlock wholesale pricing for end consumers. By focusing on essential goods and high-velocity retail categories, the platform will facilitate group-buying through a pledge-based pre-order system.

Core User Roles

Consumer

The end-user participating in pledges to receive discounted retail goods via collective action.

Retail Partner

Fulfillment nodes that manage local inventory and act as pickup or distribution points.

Platform Admin

Oversees demand aggregation, vendor negotiation, and cross-region logistics coordination.

Phase 1 Non-Negotiable Features

1

Pledge & Pre-order Engine

  • Dynamic Pricing Tiers: Price drops as total group pledge volume increases.
  • Commitment Tracking: Real-time dashboard showing progress toward the next price milestone.
  • Secure Payment Escrow: Authorized hold on funds during the pledge window.
2

Retail Node Management

  • Inventory Dashboard: For retail partners to view upcoming fulfillment batches.
  • QR Verification: Secure hand-off system for consumers picking up their items.
  • Settlement Engine: Automated commission calculation for partner storefronts.
3

Logistics & Supply Chain

  • Batch Routing: Grouping orders by geolocation for optimized bulk delivery.
  • Vendor Portal: Simplified bulk order generation based on aggregate demand.
  • Last-Mile Tracking: Visibility into batch movement from warehouse to retail node.

Success Metrics (KPIs)

Growth

Percentage of campaigns that successfully reach their minimum threshold for wholesale ordering.

Efficiency
